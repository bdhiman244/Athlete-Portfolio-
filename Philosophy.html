philosophy

Because velocity begs and my blood boils with stagnation. I run because I must, I run for the dam fun of it. Running says no to the vacuum of space, yes to the misty heights of formidable peaks. It bestows power yes, but mostly humility. Long distance running is our super power. When we run we express something primal, something which provided not just a means of survival but a path to wisdom. The test of endurance is a ruthless teacher. Be grateful for a heart that beats.

OF UTMOST IMPORTANCE

An athlete strives to render himself the perfect tool for the job, but it is of utmost importance that his striving transcends the vanity of sport.

  • THIS WILL ALSO CHANGE. All things are impermanent, triumph will pass as will your despair at its absence.
  • BE HUMBLE. The mountain remains unscathed by your ascent.
  • BE A WARRIOR.
  • LEAN INTO DISCOMFORT. Especially in mundanity..
  • HEAL UP. Make time for convalescence.
  • NATURE IS ALL THERE IS. Appropriate maximum attention to subtlety. Make obeisance to water fresh and salty, be with your people under some trees.
