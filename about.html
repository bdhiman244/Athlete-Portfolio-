I run tough mountain races not road marathons becasue they appeal to my lust for adventure. OF UTMOST IMPORTANCE

An athlete's goal is to make himself the perfect tool for the job, but it is of utmost importance that he anchors himself to some precepts which transcend the vanity of sport.

  • THIS WILL ALSO CHANGE. All things are impermanent, this triumph will pass as will your despair when its gone.
  • BE HUMBLE. The mountain always wins.
  • BE A WARRIOR.
  • ALWAYS LEAN INTO DISCOMFORT.
  • HEAL UP. Make time for convalescence.
  • NATURE IS THE BEST. Appropriate maximum attention to the subtly. Make obeisance and celebrate the beaty of the natural world with your people.
